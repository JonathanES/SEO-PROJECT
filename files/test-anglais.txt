Benjamin Pavard (French pronunciation:  born 28 March 1996) is a French footballer who plays as a defender for VfB Stuttgart and the French national team.
He began his career at Lille OSC in Ligue 1 and transferred to VfB Stuttgart in 2016, where he won the 2. Bundesliga in his first season. He made his international debut in November 2017 and played at the 2018 FIFA World Cup.
